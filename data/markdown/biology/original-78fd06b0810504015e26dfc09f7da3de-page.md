Chapter 22. Coffee Growing, Roast,
Grind, and Beans

How do you take your coffee–regular or decaf? Coffee is the most popular drink in the U.S.
However, coffee plant species growing is dominantly around the equator countries such as
Brazil, Colombia, Ethiopia, and Indonesia.

Coffee plant (Coffea sp.), native to south west Ethiopia with very little genetic diversity,
comes from beans of coffee fruit (a.k.a. berry or cherry). A combination of its susceptibility to
extreme temperature changes and considering its low genetic diversity make breeders focus
on developing more robust shade grown coffee cultivars worldwide.
